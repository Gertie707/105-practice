Oh the Places You'll Go!

I'm sure Dr. Seuss didn't know much about computers in his day but he defintely knew that new experiences make life worth living!

I never thought I would have as much fun coding as I do. Every day is an opportunity to twist and turn the learning curve into a brand new coding pretzel!

If you find pleasure in solving problems and developing a creation wrought by your own hands, you will enjoy developing your own apps! I guarantee it!

Why, What, Where, and When??? How too???

Do you like asking questions and dissecting how something came to be and then figuring how to make it even better?

Do you like pounding your head against a wall when you can't figure something out? Coding is perfect for you then! Just kidding!

While sometimes coding can be frustrating, I have learned you can't push yourself to learn something until you are ready. But it is so exciting when you finally get how something works! It is the best feeling!
